## Linux Standard Runlevels

This reference explains the standard Linux runlevels:

<details>
<summary>"0"</summary>
  
- **0** - Halt (Shuts down the system)

<summary>"1"</summary>

- **1** – Single-User Mode (Maintenance mode without networking)

<summary>"2"</summary>

- **2** – Multi-User Mode (Without networking)
</details>
